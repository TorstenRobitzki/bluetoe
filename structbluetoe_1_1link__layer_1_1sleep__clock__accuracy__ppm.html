struct bluetoe::link_layer::sleep_clock_accuracy_ppm< SleepClockAccuracyPPM, typename >

defines the sleep clock accuracy of the device hardware.

The stack uses the accuracy information to keep the time window where the slave listens for radio messages from the master, as small as possible. It's important to determine the real sleep clock accuracy. Giving to large accuracy will leed to not optimal power consumption. To small accuracy will leed to instable connections.
